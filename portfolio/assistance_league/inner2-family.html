Family Service Agency

Every ten seconds, a case of child abuse is reported in America. Children living in poverty are 45 times more likely to be victims of child abuse than their peers living just above the poverty line. Abused children are at high risk themselves for substance abuse, mental health issues and criminal behavior later in life. Fully 33% of these children will grow up to abuse their own children, creating a cycle of poverty and abuse that can last generations. Family Service Agency works to eliminate child abuse and domestic violence in impoverished families by stabilizing and strengthening at-risk families through counseling. When Los Angeles Child Services intervenes, the Assistance League of Los Angeles Family Service Agency is often their first call.

Family Service Agency offers individually tailored treatment that includes crisis intervention, individual and group counseling, womenâ€™s services and parent education that prevent and treat child abuse, domestic violence and substance abuse. Prevention services focus on positive discipline techniques, family budgeting, academic support, and enrichment and mentoring programs for youth. All services are offered in Spanish and English with some services offered in the family's home. Our goal is to offer support that keeps families safe and intact.
