Learning Center for Young Children

Our Infant-Toddler and Pre-school programs offer developmentally appropriate educational modules and practices that create a learning environment, which encompass the full spectrum of a child's development: social/emotional, cognitive, creative and physical. Our goal is to maximize each child's potential to become a fully engaged learner for life.

Children's Services offers an after-school and vacation program for school age children. In a nurturing environment we provide adult supervision and guidance through programs designed to help them have greater success in school. Children enrolled in our programs spend after-school hours and school vacation days engaged in enrichment activities including homework help, computer classes, arts and crafts, team sports and social activities.
