Assistance League of Southern California makes
a difference in nearly 95,000 people's lives every year.

Assistance League of Southern California (ALSC), a nonprofit organization founded in 1919, provides social services to 95,000 individuals with critical needs each year. The eight services of ALSC offer a full spectrum of programs such as after-school care and early education for children, cultural enrichment for youth, counseling for families, support for seniors and volunteer opportunities for the community - all of which help our clients succeed and enable them to lead independent, productive lives.
